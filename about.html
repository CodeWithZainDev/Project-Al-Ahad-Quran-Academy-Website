About Our Services

Comprehensive Quranic Education for All Ages

Learn Qaida

The foundation of Arabic reading. We use Noorani Qaida to teach correct pronunciation of Arabic letters and basic rules for beginners and children.

Quran with Tajweed

Master the art of Quranic recitation. Our tutors focus on the precise articulation points (Makharij) and rules of Tajweed to ensure beautiful and correct recitation.

Supplications and Kalmas

Essential daily Duas and the six Kalmas. We help students memorize and understand the meanings of basic Islamic supplications for daily life.

Salat and Sunnah

Learn the complete method of offering Prayer (Namaz) according to the Sunnah, including Wudu, steps of Salat, and essential Sunnah acts.

Selected Surah

Memorization and translation of frequently recited Surahs like Surah Yaseen, Surah Mulk, and others, helping students connect deeply with the message.

Hifzul Quran

A structured program for those who wish to memorize the entire Holy Quran. Our experienced Hafiz tutors provide personalized guidance and revision plans.

Our Mission

At Al Ahad Quran Academy, we strive to bridge the gap between students and the divine wisdom of the Holy Quran. Our academy was founded on the principles of excellence, accessibility, and traditional values combined with modern technology.
